Asmongold

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


Zack (born 1990/1991), better known as Asmongold, is an American Twitch streamer and YouTuber, primarily known for playing World of Warcraft.[7] He is a co-founder One True King, a group of online content creators.[8]

Asmongold first gained popularity through his YouTube channel. He has been active on Twitch since 2013, gaining a cult following as a World of Warcraft streamer.[9] Aheof the 2017 announcement of World of Warcraft Classic, he had long wished for and speculated about the game mode's development.[9] Upon the release of Classic in 2019, surged in overall popularity.[10] He was also one of the platform's most popular World of Warcraft streamers during the 2020 release of Shadowlands.[11]
            
On July 3, 2021, Asmongold started playing Final Fantasy XIV to hundreds of thousands of viewers.[12] In the weeks following, FFXIV experienced substantial growth in numbof players and overall popularity. He eventually began to also play other games like Lost Ark and Elden Ring near their time of release, and plans to return to FinFantasy XIV in the future.
            
In 2022, Asmongold started to branch out into more variety style streams. Asmongold has expressed his strong interest in continuing to branch out into creating all tdifferent types of content he enjoys.
